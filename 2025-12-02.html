When God Calls You Back
Into the Deep

Key Scripture

Let us not become weary in doing good, for at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up.

Galatians 6:9 (NIV)

After months away from recording, the Salty Talk hosts return with hearts full and voices steady, ready to recount the whirlwind of ministry that has consumed their livesnew outreaches, answered prayers, unexpected battles, and the unmistakable fingerprints of God guiding every step. What began as a simple yes to the Lord has become a growing movement of restoration, dignity, and hope for those who feel forgotten.

Testimony

The team shared how their break from podcasting was anything but restful. They stepped into new ministry territoriessetting up a fresh booth, investing hours in prayer, and taking a mission trip to Tennessee that birthed deeper connections through Christian hiphop. Doors opened for future concerts and community outreach as God continued linking them with artists who carry the heart of evangelism.

One of the most challenging yet rewarding seasons came with the development of the shower trailer. Mechanical issues, delays, and unexpected obstacles tried to shut the project down. But God moved. The trailer launched, and more than 85 people have already been given the simple gift of a hot showerrestoring dignity, peace, and the reminder that they matter. The giving closet grew alongside it, providing clothes, essentials, and compassionate care to those in need.

God continued to push them outwardtoward Austin and its growing tinyhome communities, toward new vendor events and a major auction fundraiser, toward fresh partnerships and volunteer opportunities. Though exhaustion settled heavily on their shoulders, spiritual renewal met them in every moment they whispered yes. And as they prepare for new locations, a needed truck, and the upcoming Austin outreach, they are confident that God is already ahead of them.

What God Revealed

  • God strengthens weary hands when the mission feels overwhelming.
  • Every act of dignity given to the broken mirrors the heart of Jesus.
  • Obedience opens doors no strategy ever could.
  • Community grows when believers link arms with a shared purpose.
  • God provides exactly what is needed for every new step of faith.

"When you say yes to God, Hell take you farther than you ever imaginedand Hell give you strength for every mile."

Reflection

Maybe youre feeling stretched thin. Maybe the calling God placed on your life feels too heavy, too costly, or too far beyond what you believe you can carry. Take heartGod is not asking you to walk alone. Just as He guided this ministry through exhaustion, setbacks, and miracles, He wants to meet you in your weakness and show you what He can do through a surrendered heart.

Call to Action

Take your next step with Godwhether its praying for someone, serving your community, confessing a struggle, or simply saying yes to what Hes already been nudging you toward. Pray for Salty Farm Ministries as they continue their outreach, seek resources, and prepare for upcoming trips. And ask God how He might be inviting you to join in the work of healing, dignity, and restoration.

Closing Prayer

Lord Jesus, strengthen us when we grow weary, guide us when the path feels unclear, and give us the courage to say yes to Your calling. Bless every person who longs for hope, and use us as vessels of Your love. Amen.
